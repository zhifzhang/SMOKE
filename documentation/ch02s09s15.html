2.9.15. Handle inventories that have data for multiple years

In some cases, inventories may use data from multiple years; this can occur when inventory data are not available for the modeling year of interest, but data from another year are available. SMOKE can handle this case. Unless growth factors are available to grow the available data to the desired year, the SMOKE intermediate inventory may include data from two years. Usually, using data from a nearby year is preferable to not performing the modeling at all.
