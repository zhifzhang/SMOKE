12.2. Downloading SMOKE

Steps for downloading SMOKE:

  1. Go to the CMAS Model Clearinghouse.

  2. Click on the SMOKE link in the list of CMAS-supported products.

  3. If you have already registered with CMAS, enter your email address and password. Otherwise, click the "Register now" link and complete the registration form.

  4. You will be sent to the CMAS Download Center. Select SMOKE as the product you wish to download and select your computer and compiler type.

  5. Download the installation script (smoke_install.csh), the source code, scripts, and executables archive (smoke_${version}.Linux2_x86_64pg.tar.gz), and the test case data files (smoke_${version}.nctox.MOVES.data.tar.gz). Save these files in the same directory on your machine.
