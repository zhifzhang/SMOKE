2.13.2. Reporting on the factors assigned to each source in the inventory

In addition to actually applying the growth factors, the growth processing reports the factors that were applied to each source. Cntlmat writes a report that includes the source characteristics and the factor from the /PROJECTION/ packet assigned to the source. For example, the area-source report would include the state, county, and SCCs along with the factor assigned to the source. Only those sources that have growth factors applied are included in the report. This report permits you to verify that the assignments you intended were in fact made by the program. The report is called the APROJREP, MPROJREP, or PPROJREP file for SMOKE area, mobile, and point sources, respectively.
